Why Clean Your Solar Panels?

Dirty solar panels can lose up to 30% of their energy production due to accumulated dust, pollution, leaves, and bird droppings.

Regular cleaning helps increase energy output, improve return on investment, extend panel lifespan, prevent permanent staining, and reduce maintenance costs.
